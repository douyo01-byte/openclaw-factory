Replace inline re.search calls with module-level compiled patterns and update the three patterns to accept both ASCII '#' and full-width '＃' (e.g. r"[#＃](\d+)"). Change is small and low-risk: add constants like APPROVAL_RE = re.compile(r"承認します\s*[#＃](\d+)") (and analogous patterns for diff and テストだけ) and call .search(text or "") in each parse_* function. Expected effect: correct parsing when authors use the full-width number sign common in Japanese text, and a minor performance improvement by avoiding recompiling the same regex on every call.
