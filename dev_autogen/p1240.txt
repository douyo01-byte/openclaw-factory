Wrap the requests.post call in q() with a small retry loop (e.g. 3 attempts) and incremental backoff (e.g. sleep 1s, 2s, 4s). Catch requests.exceptions.RequestException and retry; also treat HTTP 502/503/504 (and optionally 429) as transient and retry before raising. After exhausting retries, r.raise_for_status() should propagate the error as before. This is a minimal, low-risk change confined to q() and will reduce failed GraphQL calls caused by transient network or upstream API glitches, lowering unnecessary loop exits and merge skips.
