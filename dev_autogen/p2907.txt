Currently run() recompiles the same regular expressions on every inbox row (several re.match and re.sub calls inside the loop). Precompile the constant patterns once at module load (e.g. PROPOSAL_RE, OK_HOLD_RE, REQ_RE, JAP_APPROVE_RE, JAP_APPROVE_ALT_RE, JAP_HOLD_RE, JAP_QUESTION_RE and SPACE_NORMALIZE_RE) and reuse them inside the loop. This is a minimal, low-risk change: move pattern.compile calls to top-level constants and replace re.match/re.sub with compiled_pattern.match / compiled_pattern.sub. Expected benefits: measurable CPU/time savings when processing batches of rows (especially for large queues) and clearer centralization of the patterns for future tweaks.
