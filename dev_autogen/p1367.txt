In _load_persona_from_env(), replace open(core, "r", encoding="utf-8").read().strip() and open(role, "r", encoding="utf-8").read().strip() with with open(core, "r", encoding="utf-8") as f: t.append(f.read().strip()) (and likewise for role). This ensures file descriptors are closed, avoids ResourceWarning/file-handle leaks in long-running or repeated runs, and improves reliability with no behavior change to returned persona text.
