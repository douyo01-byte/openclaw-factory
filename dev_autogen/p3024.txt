The score() function accumulates weights from keywords and decision_patterns but does not bound the resulting priority. That can produce out-of-range and surprising priority values (and affect decision thresholds). Add a single-line clamp (priority = max(0.0, min(priority, 1.0))) immediately after weight accumulation and before mapping to a decision and before returning/storing. This is a minimal, low-risk change: it keeps existing scoring logic but enforces sensible bounds, ensures stored priority values are predictable, and prevents extreme weights from skewing downstream behavior.
