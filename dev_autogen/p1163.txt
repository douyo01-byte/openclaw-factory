Call PRAGMA foreign_keys = ON on the sqlite3 connection before executing the DDL. The file declares a FOREIGN KEY from dev_events.proposal_id to dev_proposals(id) but SQLite does not enforce foreign keys by default. Adding a single line such as conn.execute('PRAGMA foreign_keys = ON') immediately after opening the connection ensures referential integrity (prevents inserting events referencing non-existent proposals) and avoids latent data-consistency bugs. This is a small, low-risk change confined to apply().
