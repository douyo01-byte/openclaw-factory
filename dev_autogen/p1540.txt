batch=1540-1540

Modify fetch_text to perform one additional attempt when a fetch fails due to a network exception, a 5xx HTTP status, or 429 Too Many Requests. On the first failure sleep a small backoff (e.g. 1.0s) and retry once, returning the same (text, status, err) tuple semantics. Include the attempt count or retry hint in the err string (e.g. "exc:Timeout:...;retry=1" or "http_503;retry=1") so callers and logs can distinguish transient vs permanent failures. Keep the change minimal (one retry only) to reduce risk and avoid altering upstream behavior, and preserve TIMEOUT and headers.
