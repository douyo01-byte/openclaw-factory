These helper functions call conn.execute(...) but do not call conn.commit(), so inserts/updates may not be persisted immediately (or may be lost on process crash). Add a single conn.commit() after the execute in each of set_ctx_last_item, enqueue_contact, and upsert_role_brief. Optionally, add conn.commit() after any chat_jobs status update in main (e.g. after UPDATE ... SET status='error' / other updates) so job-state changes are saved. Expected effect: database writes (bot state, contacts, role briefs, job status) will be reliably persisted, preventing lost state and making subsequent runs/other processes see the updates. This is a small, low-risk change (one-line additions) with no change to read logic or external behavior beyond ensuring durability.
