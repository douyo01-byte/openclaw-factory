enqueue_chat_job currently executes an INSERT into chat_jobs but does not commit the transaction. If the caller doesn't commit, newly enqueued jobs may not be visible to other connections or may be lost on process exit. Add conn.commit() immediately after conn.execute(...) in enqueue_chat_job (or return a convenience wrapper that commits) so that chat job rows are persisted right away. Expected effect: improved reliability — enqueued chat jobs become visible to other DB clients/processes and are not lost, with minimal, low-risk change.
